
> 1. Introducing Your Idea - Present your proposal to the group

- I’d like to suggest… because… 
- My idea is… and it could benefit… 
- How does everyone feel about…? 
- One option could be… which would allow… 
- I think it would be a good idea to… 
- I propose that we…

> 2. Asking for Opinions / Encouraging Participation - Involve everyone in the discussion

- What do you think about…? 
- Do you agree that…? 
- Could you share your thoughts on…? 
- How would you feel if we…? 
- Would you be happy with…? 
- Does anyone have any other suggestions?

> 3. Identifying Areas of Agreement - Find common ground

- I think we all agree that… 
- It seems we all want… 
- None of us want… 
- We all agree that the main goal is… 
- It looks like we share the same priority of…

> 4. Summarising and Closing - Confirm decisions and finish

- So, our plan could be… 
- That seems like a reasonable compromise. 
- Thanks, everyone, for your input! 
- To summarise, we’ve decided that… 
- Let’s move forward with this plan. 
- I think we have a solution that works for everyone.

> Expressing personal opinions

- Personally, I think/feel/believe that … 
- In my opinion/view, … 
- From my perspective/my point of view, … 
- As far as I’m concerned/aware, … 
- If you ask me, …

> Expressing agreement

- I couldn’t agree more. 
- That’s a good/fair point. 
- I take/see your point. 
- That makes sense.

> Expressing disagreement

- I’m not sure. 
- But on the other hand, … 
- I completely disagree with you.
 
> Partially agreeing

- I agree (with you) up to a point, but ... 
- I agree to some extent, but ... 
- I suppose so, but ... 
- I understand what you’re saying, but ... 
- That’s a fair point, although it could also be argued that ...
